🔒 Privacy Policy

Aasan ERP — Last updated: March 2026

Your business data belongs to you and only you. We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party, or external organization.

What Data We Collect

When you create an account, we store your email address and full name to identify your account. All business data you enter — products, sales, purchases, customers, suppliers, and expenses — is stored securely in your private account and is only accessible by you.

How Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored in a secure cloud database (Supabase) with Row Level Security enabled. This means it is technically impossible for any other user of this app to access your data.

Who Can See Your Data

Only you. We do not share your business data with any third-party.:

FBR & Tax Authorities

This application is a business management tool. It is not connected to FBR, any POS integration system, or any government reporting infrastructure. We do not automatically report any of your business transactions to any authority.

If a government authority ever contacts us with a valid court order regarding a specific account, we are legally required to comply. However, we will always notify the account holder where legally permitted to do so.

Your Right to Delete Your Data

You can permanently delete all your business data at any time from Settings → Danger Zone → Delete All My Data. This action immediately and permanently removes all your records from our database and cannot be undone.

To delete your account entirely, contact us via WhatsApp 0326-6450963

Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS encrypted connections. Your password is never stored in plain text. We use Supabase's enterprise-grade security infrastructure which is used by thousands of companies worldwide.

Cookies & Tracking

We do not use advertising cookies. We do not track your behavior for marketing purposes. We do not display any advertisements. The only data stored locally on your device is your login session so you don't have to sign in every time.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy in a way that affects your data rights, we will notify you via a banner inside the app before the change takes effect.

Contact

If you have any questions about your data or this privacy policy, contact us on WhatsApp 0326-6450963

This policy applies to all users of Aasan ERP operating in Pakistan.
